DOG SECTION

Our primary service objective is to guard our clients’ assets, resources, and public image. Oneguard Security is the first choice when you require safety, reassurance, and reliability because of our dedication.
 
A representative of our management team will visit each site before to each contract and conduct a risk assessment to assure that our clients’ expectations and requirements are met to the highest of standards.
Oneguard Security ensures that all handler and thier dogs have training up to date and are members of the following association.
 
  • NASDU (National association of security dog users)
  • NTIPDU (National training inspectorate for professional dog users)
  • BIPDT (British institute of professional dog trainers)

SEARCH DOGS

To detect unlawful substances such as drugs, explosives, or excessive volumes of cigarettes, we utilise detection dogs. Detection dogs have become a crucial asset for police, the army, and security in recent years.
 
Oneguard Security’s Search Dog Team can be deployed to minimize the threat of an explosives-based attack in a variety of situations. Entry point inspections, car sweeps, and building searches can be done as often as needed to provide you and your employees the peace of mind they need to go about their daily tasks without fear.

STATIC GUARD

The use of a uniformed security guard to offer protection for property and premises is referred to as static security. Our static security guards go further than their job role, objecting the passive and immobile in favour of actively participating through frequent and thorough patrols that vary in time and route to ensure unpredictability in the event of an incident, which will be instantly logged in an incident book and reported.
